Spoken English with Basic Computer Training Course Overview

Welcome to the Spoken English with Basic Computer Training course at Info-Tech Computer Training Center! This course is designed for individuals who want to improve their English communication skills while also gaining essential computer knowledge. Perfect for beginners, this course will help you build confidence in both spoken English and basic computer operations.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Spoken English: Start with the basics of spoken English. Learn essential vocabulary, common phrases, and everyday expressions. Understand the importance of pronunciation, intonation, and accent in effective communication.
  • Grammar and Sentence Structure: Improve your grammar and sentence structure. Learn about tenses, parts of speech, and the construction of simple, compound, and complex sentences. Practice forming correct and coherent sentences.
  • Listening and Speaking Skills: Enhance your listening and speaking skills through interactive exercises and practical activities. Participate in group discussions, role-plays, and presentations to improve your fluency and confidence in speaking English.
  • Reading and Comprehension: Develop your reading and comprehension skills. Read various texts, including articles, stories, and reports, and practice summarizing and discussing their content. Improve your ability to understand and interpret written English.
  • Writing Skills: Learn to write effectively in English. Practice writing emails, letters, reports, and essays. Focus on clarity, coherence, and proper formatting. Get feedback on your writing to help you improve.
  • Basic Computer Skills: Gain essential computer skills needed in today's digital world. Learn how to operate a personal computer, navigate the operating system, and manage files and folders. Understand the basics of hardware and software.
  • Internet and Email Usage: Learn how to use the internet and email effectively. Understand how to browse the web, perform online searches, and communicate via email. Learn about internet safety and how to protect your personal information online.
  • Introduction to Office Applications: Get introduced to basic office applications such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Learn how to create documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. Understand how these tools can be used in various professional and personal contexts.
